About Us

Who We Are

Below are three brief descriptions of who Penguin Articles is, in both professional and personal point of views.

Our History So Far

Throughout our history, Penguin's covered many stories, both personal and professional. However, as individuals and an article company we've made sure to always ask permission before publishing a story, even before we established ourselves as a professional group of writers.

Our Work's Purpose

We've said it many times before and will eventually do so again. Penguin Articles is what we call ourselves, and what we do is cover stories and events taking place around the local schools. But why is it we do what we do here? Could it be that we want to bring awareness to parents and other caregivers that may not know all the goings on? Or is it to expose some deep secret behind closed doors in these schools? Simply put, we enjoy writing and listening! We take passion in hearing about other's experiences and letting them know we're here for them, as we want to make their concerns known to all, should they desire.

Who our crew is

Originally, we were just high school students that had problems of our own. We all became friends through sharing our struggles and lending a hand to one another, which then came the idea of "If we could do this for other students, maybe they won't feel so left out or alone" giving rise to what we truly wanted to do, share stories! So from then on, we began to write stories about other's experiences, to hopefully let other struggling students know they're not alone in what they're dealing with.
